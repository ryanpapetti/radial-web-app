

# VHosts for projects

<VirtualHost *:80>
    ServerName radial-app.com
		ProxyPreserveHost On
		ProxyRequests Off
		ProxyPass / http://localhost:9000/
		ProxyPassReverse / http://localhost:9000/
		
		ErrorLog ${APACHE_LOG_DIR}/error.log
    CustomLog ${APACHE_LOG_DIR}/access.log combined
</VirtualHost>
